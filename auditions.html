Auditions

We've concluded auditions for the Fall 2014 semester. Come back in the spring!

P5 auditions emphasize sight-reading, blending, pitch, and ability to hold one's part through vocal exercises and individual and group sight-reading tasks. Auditionees are also asked to prepare a short solo, preferably in the classical style, for the audition. We may have a callback audition to hear more on blend and part-holding.

We are always looking for experienced singers, and encourage all who are interested to audition! If you have scheduled an UCCE general audition, please indicate on the form that you are interested in P5. If you have any questions, please feel free to email our business manager.
